2 · Demographics

The most important onboarding page per client. Everything downstream (cohort filters, percentile math, drug rankings, price comparisons) pivots on these fields.

Required
Age · Sex · City + State · Height (ft+in) · Starting weight · Health history (multi-select chips incl. T2D, PCOS, pancreatitis, thyroid, None).

The landing hub after onboarding. Every path either starts here or returns here.

Header band

Blocks

8b · Other options

Observational cohort outcomes per GLP-1 drug — not a recommendation. Rankings, "best efficacy/tolerability/balance" badges, and "data-driven recommendation" language are all removed to stay clear of medical-advice territory.

Hero
"What others in your cohort experienced" — "Individual results vary — this is not a recommendation. Your prescriber decides what's right for you."
Drug cards
No rank circle. Brand + generic + CURRENT tag if applicable · 3 stats (Weight Δ · Side fx · Rating). 52-week window.

Logging · /log-dose

12 · Log Dose (or Pill)

Fast data capture for a taken dose. Injection-site vs pill "taken with" swap happens automatically based on the current regimen's form.

Core fields
Date · Time · Injection site (abdomen / thigh / upper arm) or Taken with (empty stomach / with food / after food) · Titration step (steady / stepped up / stepped down) · Dose event (on time / took late / skipped side fx / skipped other) · Notes.
Optional check-in
10 compact factor rows (6 baseline + 4 GLP-1 extras — food tolerance, hydration, muscle / strength, cycle regularity).
Submit
"Record dose" or "Record pill" (form-aware) → success → auto-return to /dashboard.
Logging · /log-side-effect

13 · Log Side Effect

Single-event capture with enough granularity to feed Insights trend cards. Includes an escalation callout for severe ongoing symptoms.

Required
Category · Severity 1–5.
Optional
Duration · Impact · Date · Time.
Escalation
When severity ≥ 4 AND duration = Ongoing, a red-bordered callout lists pancreatitis / gallbladder / thyroid / dehydration red-flag symptoms and references 911.
Logging · /log-cost

14 · Log Cost

Tracks actual spend. 30-day average card up top labeled "post-insurance copay" so nobody confuses the $45 figure with list price.

Fields
Amount · Cost type (Copay / OOP / Coupon / Retail) · Pharmacy · Insurance applied · Supply duration · Fill date.
Logging · /log-weight (now documented)

15 · Log Weight

Per-weigh-in capture. Feeds both Dashboard and Insights weight-trend area charts and the Comparison weight-change outcome row.

Fields
Weight (lb / kg toggle) · Date · Time · Fasted? · Notes. Header shows current weight, Δ from start, Δ from previous entry.
Schema
weight_entries table stores weight_lb · recorded_at (timestamptz) · fasted · notes · source (manual / apple_health / google_fit / smart_scale).

Cross-cutting

Patterns used across screens

CohortBadge

Reusable "YOUR MATCHED COHORT · n=1,247" strip with filter chips and an amber "Illustrative demo data · not clinical evidence" footer. Appears at the top of Comparison and (when clinician view is off) Insights.

SectionHeader

Uppercase eyebrow + title + optional right-aligned meta (e.g., 52-wk window, de-identified). Clinical tone marker across every main-app card.

1–5 rating pattern

Every factor rating (baselines, check-ins, side-effect severity) renders as 5 equal flex-1 buttons with descriptive single-word endpoints (Drained / Energized, Mild / Severe, etc.) centered under the 1 and 5 buttons in bold brand blue. Convention: 5 = more of the named factor.

Return-to-dashboard on submit

All five log screens render a brief success confirmation and auto-navigate back to /dashboard so the user never has to back-button out of a log entry.

Category-aware dashboard

Today's eyebrow reads "TRIAL WEAVE · GLP-1". When new categories ship, the eyebrow and the current-regimen card swap; the rest of the chrome is unchanged.

Illustrative-demo-data disclaimer

Until the real cohort n is wired up, every cohort stat renders under an amber "Illustrative demo data · not clinical evidence" footer on CohortBadge and a softened "Individual results vary — not a prediction of your outcome" line on Insights.
